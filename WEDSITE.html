Graphics AND Animation

"I remember the first time I played around with creating pictures on a computer. At first, it was just simple shapes and colors, but then I learned something amazing: with animation, I could make them move. Imagine this: I could take a drawing of a character and make it blink, walk, even dance! It was like turning a still picture into a living, breathing world. I got so hooked that I started creating entire scenes—trees swaying, birds flying, and characters having conversations. Every time I added movement, it felt like magic—like I could actually bring my imagination to life. And that’s the beauty of animation and graphics: it’s not just about making things look good, it’s about making them feel real and alive."
